About

I’m an education researcher and data analyst focused on helping students learn to read and write longer, more complex words. My work centers on reading and writing interventions, data-based decision making, and practical tools that support teachers’ instruction. Methodologically, I work across randomized trials and meta-analyses, multilevel and SEM approaches (often in R), and build analysis workflows and dashboards with reproducible code.

I designed SHINE, a multisyllabic spelling intervention for students in grades 3–5, and collaborate on projects that connect assessment data to instruction at scale.
